# US-retail-expansion-investment 

For a national apparel retailer, which U.S. states represent the strongest opportunities for store expansion, inventory redistribution and marketing investment based on retail sales growth and economic stability? 
	
	The phrase “strongest opportunities” can be further distilled into the following research questions: 
Which US states are experiencing the most revenue growth? 
Which states have the most potential, having low unemployment and high revenue growth?
Which states would be a bad investment, having high unemployment and low revenue growth? 

This project benefits stakeholders by providing a multi-dimensional framework for investment and resource allocation. Instead of viewing the United States as a monolith, this analysis utilizes data abstraction to categorize states into strategic zones, allowing for more nuanced decision-making that benefits both business and the labor force.
